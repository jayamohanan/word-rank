About Word Frequency

This game is based on the COCA_WordFrequency list, downloaded from a public website. The list ranks English words by how often they are used in real-world texts.

"Most frequently used" means the word appears more often in English writing and speech, according to the COCA (Corpus of Contemporary American English) database.
